What We Offer

Built by students,
for students

We connect struggling students with peer tutors who recently mastered the same material — creating a relatable, pressure-free learning experience.

Student-Led

Our tutors are peers who have recently conquered the same concepts, so they know exactly where you get stuck.

Community Focused

Proudly serving the Santa Clarita valley and surrounding areas with both virtual and in-person sessions.

Free & Accessible

Quality education should never come with a price tag. MathMinds is free — always has been, always will be.

How It Works

Three steps to
better grades

Getting started is simple, fast, and completely free.

01

Sign Up

Fill out our simple form with your current math level, specific struggles, and goals for the semester.

02

Get Matched

Our team reviews your submission and connects you with a peer tutor who has recently excelled in that exact subject.

03

Start Learning

Coordinate directly with your tutor for regular virtual or in-person sessions. Build confidence at your own pace.
