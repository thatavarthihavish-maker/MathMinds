Who Are We?

MathMinds was founded by Havish Thatavarthi and passionate students in Santa Clarita who recognized a critical need: bridging the gap in math education through relatable, peer-to-peer mentorship.

Since our establishment in December 2025, we have been on a mission to build confidence and conceptual understanding in our scholars without the pressure of traditional grading environments.
